☀️ THE SUN

Quick Facts:

  • Type: Star
  • Diameter: 1.3927 million km
  • Temperature: At surface: 5,600°C / 10,000°F
  • Age: 4.6 billion years
  • Mass: 99.86% of the total solar system mass

About

The Sun orbits the Galactic Center at a distance of 24,000 to 28,000 light-years. Its distance from Earth defines the astronomical unit (AU), which is about 149.6 million km or ~8 light-minutes. The Sun is ~109 times wider than Earth and about 330,000 times more massive, made mostly of hydrogen (~73%) and helium (~25%).

The Sun is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma. Its energy comes from nuclear fusion in its core. It radiates mostly visible light and infrared energy. It’s the most vital energy source for life on Earth and has been studied and revered since ancient times.
