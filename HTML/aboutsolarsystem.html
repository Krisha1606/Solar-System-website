About Solar System Explorer

Solar System Explorer is an interactive and educational website designed to help users of all ages learn about the fascinating objects in our solar system. From the rocky inner planets to the gas giants and beyond, this site offers an immersive space experience right from your browser.

Features

  • Separate pages for each planet
  • High-resolution background images
  • Smooth transitions and navigation
  • Fun facts and quizzes
  • Clear categorization of inner and outer planets

Our Mission

We created this project to make space exploration fun and accessible to everyone. Whether you're a student, a teacher, or just curious about the universe, Solar System Explorer provides a visually engaging way to explore the planets and learn amazing facts about them.

How to Explore

Use the navigation menu or planet buttons to jump between planets. You can also click the left/right arrows on each page to slide smoothly through the planets in order. Each page provides an image and key information about that celestial body.
